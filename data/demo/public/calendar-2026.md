# 2026 Academic Calendar

Horizonte Community School uses a two-semester calendar for middle and high
school.

- First semester classes run from February 9 to June 26, 2026.
- Midyear recess runs from June 29 to July 12, 2026.
- Second semester classes run from July 13 to December 11, 2026.
- Recovery exam week for first semester subjects is June 22 to June 26, 2026.
- Final recovery exam week is December 7 to December 11, 2026.
- Parent-teacher conferences happen on April 18, August 22, and November 14.

Families should use the family portal for individual schedules and room changes.
