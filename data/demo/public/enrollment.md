# Enrollment And Reenrollment

Families can complete enrollment through the school office or the family portal.
The synthetic demo process requires:

- student identification document;
- guardian identification document;
- proof of residence issued in the last 90 days;
- previous school transcript for transfer students;
- signed digital services consent form;
- health and emergency contact form.

Enrollment support is available Monday to Friday from 8:00 to 17:00. Families
without stable internet can request an assisted kiosk session at the school
office.
