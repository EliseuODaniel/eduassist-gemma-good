# Recovery Exam and Family Support Notice

The first semester recovery exam week runs from August 12, 2026 to August 16, 2026.

Families should confirm missing activities before August 8, 2026.

Required documents for office support:

- Student ID or guardian photo ID.
- Latest school notebook or teacher feedback sheet.
- Medical certificate if the absence was health related.

The school office will provide in-person support from 08:00 to 12:00 on weekdays.

Families without internet can visit the secretary desk or ask the support channel
to print the recovery checklist.
